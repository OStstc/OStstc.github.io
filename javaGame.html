Gameplay footage of Grackal the Crow

Grackal the Crow

This was one of my favorite assignments to do as a project. The project started off as a template with a moving character, coins spawning, tiles, and a score. All of the sprites were done by me, I've gained a lot more experience with sprite art since then but I still find the sprites endearing.

Each enemy has unqiue behaviour in how they move, the ghost moves diagonally, the spider moves towards the player while also moving randomly, and the blob goes straight for the player. There is dynamic difficulty, as the enemies move faster the longer the game goes on for.
